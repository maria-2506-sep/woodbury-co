Our Story

Woodbury & Co. started as a small dream of creating a café that feels like home. Our goal is to bring people together over freshly brewed coffee and delicious food in a relaxing environment.

Every cup is prepared with carefully selected beans, and every dessert is baked with love. Whether you're meeting friends, working remotely, or enjoying a quiet moment alone, Woodbury & Co. welcomes you.

Our Mission

To create a cozy space where great coffee, good food, and meaningful conversations come together.
